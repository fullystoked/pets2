Our Screening process

How we identify the ideal home for our puppies

Ensuring or puppies go to the right home is a high priority and we screen all of our puppy buyers thoroughly.

The types of people and homes we are looking for are:

  1. Prepared to wait for a puppy
  2. Experienced with the breed
  3. Have sufficient budget
  4. Have an adequate and safe environment
  5. Have the capacity to exercise the breed sufficiently and appropriately
  6. Have an understanding of the grooming requirents of the breed
  7. Willin and able to visit and get to know us

What to expect

As we receive a lot of enquiries, please note the steps in our process:

  1. Please do your research on the breed
  2. Join our waitlist
  3. Have a chat and make sure this breed is for you
  4. Meet me in person
  5. Visit and meet puppies (from 6 weeks old)
  6. Collect and pay for your puppy (when pups are 8 weeks old)

We like to meet and get to know our puppy buyers, so please only contact us if you can come and meet us in Newcastle.
